COMPARISON

Artifacts in MRI are unwanted signals or distortions. Comparison involves analyzing multiple artifacts to identify:

  • Type & severity of each artifact
  • Sources causing them (hardware, motion, or acquisition settings)
  • Impact on image quality

This helps in prioritizing corrections, improving image quality, and standardizing artifact documentation across projects.
