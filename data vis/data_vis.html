DATA VISUALISATION

Data visualization transforms raw ASL MRI data into interpretable images and metrics. In the AURA context, it allows users to:

  • Upload MRI datasets and immediately visualize axial, coronal, or sagittal slices
  • Display mean images, variability maps, and voxel-level signal changes
  • Highlight regions affected by artifacts using heatmaps and color-coded overlays
  • Enable interactive exploration, helping detect signal instability, motion, or distortion

This approach bridges quantitative data with visual intuition, making artifact detection more transparent and accessible.
