Arterial Transit Artifact

Artifact Name

Arterial Transit Artifact

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Physiological

Short Description

Arterial transit artifact occurs when labeled blood has not yet reached the tissue at the time of imaging.

How It Appears in Image

Bright signals appear along arteries instead of within the brain tissue.

Primary Cause

Delayed arterial transit time or improper post-labeling delay settings.

Clinical Impact

Can lead to overestimation of perfusion or misinterpretation of vascular signals.

Severity Level

Moderate

Common Fix

Adjust post-labeling delay or use multi-delay ASL acquisition techniques.
