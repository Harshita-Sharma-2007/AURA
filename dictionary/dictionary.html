DICTIONARY

The Dictionary module focuses on building a structured and standardized artifact dictionary for ASL MRI. Unlike informal notes or ad-hoc documentation, this module:

  • Enforces a uniform structure across all artifacts
  • Is version-controlled and reproducible, ensuring consistency
  • Separates domain knowledge from code, enabling reuse across projects
  • Is automation-ready, not just descriptive
  • Acts as a knowledge layer that can integrate into quality control (QC) pipelines

Use Cases:

  • Automated QC systems for MRI data
  • Benchmarking and validation workflows
  • Educational and documentation purposes
  • Future machine-readable transformations (JSON/YAML)
