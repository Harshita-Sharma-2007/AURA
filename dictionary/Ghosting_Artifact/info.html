Ghosting Artifact

Artifact Name

Ghosting Artifact

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Motion

Short Description

Ghosting artifacts occur when moving structures create repeated or shifted copies of the image.

How It Appears in Image

Replicated “ghost” structures appear along the phase-encoding direction.

Primary Cause

Patient motion, blood flow, or physiological movements such as breathing or heartbeat.

Clinical Impact

Reduces clarity of perfusion maps and may obscure diagnostic features.

Severity Level

Moderate

Common Fix

Use motion correction methods and instruct patients to remain still during scanning.
