Motion Artifact

Artifact Name

Motion Artifact

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Motion

Short Description

Motion artifact occurs when the patient moves during the ASL scan, causing misalignment between images and unreliable perfusion values.

How It Appears in Image

Motion artifact appears as blurring, ghosting, or misaligned brain structures across ASL images, especially between control and label images.

Primary Cause

Patient movement during scan acquisition, especially between label and control images.

Clinical Impact

Motion artifact can lead to incorrect cerebral blood flow values, reducing the reliability of ASL results.

Severity Level

Moderate to Severe

Common Fix

Instruct patient to stay still, use motion correction during preprocessing, and reject scans with excessive motion.
