Signal Dropout Artifact

Artifact Name

Signal Dropout Artifact

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Magnetic Field Artifact

Short Description

Signal dropout occurs when local magnetic field variations cause a complete loss of signal in certain image regions.

How It Appears in Image

Dark areas with little or no signal, often near sinuses or air cavities.

Primary Cause

Magnetic susceptibility differences between tissues and air.

Clinical Impact

Important anatomical or perfusion information may be lost.

Severity Level

Moderate

Common Fix

Adjust echo time or use sequences less sensitive to susceptibility effects.
