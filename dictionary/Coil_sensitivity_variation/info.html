Coil Sensitivity Variation

Artifact Name

Coil Sensitivity Variation

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Hardware

Short Description

Signal intensity variation caused by uneven sensitivity of multi-channel receiver coils.

How It Appears in Image

Gradual intensity shading across the image, often brighter near the coil and darker farther away.

Primary Cause

Non-uniform radiofrequency coil sensitivity profile.

Clinical Impact

Misinterpretation of perfusion asymmetry and biased quantitative measurements.

Severity Level

Moderate

Common Fix

Apply coil sensitivity correction or intensity normalization during preprocessing.
