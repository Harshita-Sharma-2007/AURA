Labeling Failure

Artifact Name

Labeling Failure

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Technical

Short Description

Labeling failure occurs when arterial blood is not properly magnetically labeled.

How It Appears in Image

Very low or absent perfusion signal across the entire brain.

Primary Cause

Incorrect placement of labeling plane or hardware malfunction.

Clinical Impact

Invalid cerebral blood flow quantification.

Severity Level

Severe

Common Fix

Reposition labeling plane and verify labeling pulse parameters.
