B₀ Inhomogeneity Artifact

Artifact Name

B₀ Inhomogeneity

Modality

ASL MRI

Category

Magnetic Field Artifact

Short Description

B₀ inhomogeneity occurs when the main magnetic field inside the MRI scanner is not perfectly uniform across the imaging region.

How It Appears in Image

Images may show signal loss, distortion, or spatial misalignment, especially near air–tissue interfaces such as sinuses.

Primary Cause

Magnetic susceptibility differences between tissues or improper magnetic field shimming.

Clinical Impact

May lead to inaccurate localization of perfusion signals and distortion of anatomical structures.

Severity Level

Moderate

Common Fix

Apply proper shimming and use field correction techniques during image acquisition or reconstruction.
