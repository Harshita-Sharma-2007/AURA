AURA Platform

AURA is an innovative platform focused on building impactful tech solutions through collaboration. It contributes to OSIPI (Open Science Initiative for Perfusion Imaging), supporting open research and advancements in medical imaging.

DICTIONARY
Medical terms simplified. Quickly understand perfusion imaging, MRI, and CT concepts.
SEARCH ENGINE
Smart search with filters, AND/OR logic, and regex for precise results.
UPLOAD SYSTEM
Upload medical data securely and collaborate easily with others.
DIAGNOSIS TOOL
Analyze imaging data and detect patterns or abnormalities.
COMPARISON MODE
Compare artifacts side-by-side to improve accuracy.
DATA VISUALISATION
Upload MRI and get pictorial representation of MRI.
