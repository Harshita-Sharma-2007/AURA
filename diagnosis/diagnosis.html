DIAGNOSIS

Artifact Diagnosis is the process of identifying, categorizing, and understanding unwanted signals or distortions in MRI data. Key steps include:

  • Visual inspection of MRI slices to spot anomalies
  • Voxel-level analysis to detect signal inconsistencies
  • Comparison across datasets to identify patterns
  • Linking artifacts to causes (hardware, motion, acquisition settings)
  • Supports automated Quality Control (QC) by providing structured knowledge for later analysis

Use: Helps researchers and clinicians detect, document, and correct artifacts, ensuring higher data quality and reproducibility.
